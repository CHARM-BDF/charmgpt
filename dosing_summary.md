# NAC Dosing Summary from Pediatric Clinical Trials

## Current Studies - Dosing Information

### 1. NCT04916080 - Chronic Kidney Disease
- **Dose:** 10 mg/kg/day, orally (10mg/Kg/12 hours)
- **Age Range:** 6 Months - 18 Years
- **Duration:** 3 months
- **Route:** Oral

### 2. NCT02080182 - Pediatric Acute Pyelonephritis
- **Dose:** Weight-based dosing:
  - >30 kg: 1.5 tablets daily (900 mg)
  - 8.5-30 kg: 1 tablet daily (600 mg)
  - <8.5 kg: 70 mg/kg
- **Age Range:** 1 Year - 16 Years
- **Duration:** 5 days
- **Route:** Oral (effervescent tablets)
- **Formulation:** 600 mg effervescent tablets

### 3. NCT05611086 - Pediatric Leukemia
- **Dose:** 600 mg/day (capsules)
- **Age Range:** 1 Year - 10 Years
- **Duration:** 6 weeks (during induction chemotherapy)
- **Route:** Oral (capsules)
- **Other Names:** Fluimucil

### 4. NCT02168387 - Atelectasis
- **Dose:** Not specified in detail (used as mucolytic agent)
- **Age Range:** Up to 18 Years
- **Duration:** 48 hours (alternating with dornase alfa every 6 hours)
- **Route:** Likely inhaled/nebulized (mucolytic use)

### 5. NCT00248625 - Non-Acetaminophen Pediatric Acute Liver Failure
- **Dose:** 150 mg/kg/day
- **Age Range:** Up to 18 Years
- **Duration:** Up to 7 consecutive days
- **Route:** Intravenous (continuous infusion over 24 hours)
- **Formulation:** In 5% dextrose (D5W)
- **Other Names:** Mucomyst

### 6. NCT01172275 - Pediatric Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder
- **Dose:** Titrated dosing:
  - Week 1: 900 mg once daily
  - Week 2: 900 mg twice daily
  - Weeks 3-12: 900 mg three times daily (2700 mg/day)
- **Age Range:** 8 Years - 17 Years
- **Duration:** 12 weeks
- **Route:** Oral (effervescent tablets)
- **Other Names:** PharmaNAC

### 7. NCT00993265 - Pediatric Trichotillomania
- **Dose:** 2400 mg/day (1200 mg AM, 1200 mg PM)
- **Age Range:** 8 Years - 17 Years
- **Duration:** 12 weeks
- **Route:** Oral (capsules)

## Dosing Patterns by Route

### Oral Dosing:
- **Low dose:** 10 mg/kg/day (CKD)
- **Medium dose:** 600-900 mg/day (leukemia, pyelonephritis)
- **High dose:** 2400-2700 mg/day (OCD, trichotillomania)
- **Weight-based:** 70 mg/kg for <8.5 kg (pyelonephritis)

### Intravenous Dosing:
- **Standard:** 150 mg/kg/day (liver failure)
- **Infusion:** Continuous over 24 hours

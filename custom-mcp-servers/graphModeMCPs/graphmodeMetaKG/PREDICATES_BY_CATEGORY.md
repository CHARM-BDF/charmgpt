## Key Insights

### Most Common Predicates Across All Categories:
1. **`related_to`** - General relationship (appears in all categories)
2. **`affects`** - Causal/functional relationships
3. **`interacts_with`** - Molecular interactions
4. **`subclass_of`** - Hierarchical relationships
5. **`located_in`** - Spatial relationships
6. **`coexists_with`** - Co-occurrence relationships
7. **`disrupts`** - Disruptive effects
8. **`treats_or_applied_or_studied_to_treat`** - Therapeutic relationships
9. **`close_match`** - Similarity relationships
10. **`produces`** - Production relationships

### Category-Specific Patterns:

- **Protein/Polypeptide**: Heavy use of `affects`, `interacts_with`, `produces`
- **SmallMolecule**: High `affects` count, many therapeutic relationships
- **DiseaseOrPhenotypicFeature**: Many `subclass_of`, `manifestation_of`, `precedes`
- **AnatomicalEntity**: Strong `has_part`, `location_of` relationships
- **Gene**: High `produces` (gene â†’ gene product relationships)
- **ProteinFamily**: Very limited predicates (only 4 total)

### Therapeutic Focus:
Many categories show strong therapeutic relationships:
- `treats_or_applied_or_studied_to_treat`
- `preventative_for_condition`
- `predisposes_to_condition`
- `biomarker_for`

This suggests the SmartAPI MetaKG is heavily focused on biomedical applications and drug discovery.
